The best movies I ever seen

Top 3

Avatar: The Way of Water (2022)

After taking on the form of an avatar, soldier Jake Sully becomes the leader of the Na'vi people and takes on the mission of protecting his new friends from the greedy businessmen of Earth. Now he has someone to fight for - with Jake, his beautiful lover Neytiri. When the heavily armed earthlings return to Pandora, Jake is ready to fight them off.

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone (2001)

The life of ten-year-old Harry Potter cannot be called sweet: his parents died when he was barely a year old, and from his uncle and aunt, who took the orphan into their care, he only gets jabs and slaps. But on Harry's eleventh birthday, everything changes. A strange guest, who unexpectedly appears on the doorstep, brings a letter from which the boy learns that he is actually a wizard and has been enrolled in a school of magic called Hogwarts. And in just a couple of weeks, Harry will be rushing on the Hogwarts Express train towards a new life, where incredible adventures, loyal friends and, most importantly, the key to solving the mystery of his parents' death await him.

The Hobbit: An Unexpected Journey (2012)

The hobbit Bilbo Baggins sets out on a grand quest to reclaim the lost dwarven kingdom of Erebor from the dragon Smaug. Quite unexpectedly, the wizard Gandalf the Grey makes contact with the hobbit. Thus, Bilbo finds himself and joins a company of thirteen dwarves led by the legendary warrior Thorin Oakenshield. Their journey will take them through the Wild, a treacherous land inhabited by goblins and orcs, deadly wargs, giant spiders, shapeshifting folk, and wizards.
